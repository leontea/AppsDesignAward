Apps Design Award

Best Fan's Choice

As it’s known to all that a good product is not necessary equal to a fan’s choice, and it’s definely quite hard to draw an equality between. Now, Power Remte, an elegant and the most stable app has managed to make it. Exclusive to iPhone and iPad, Power Remote is a clean practical PowerPoint/Keynote/Google Slide remote control tool for presentation. With a simple but elegant design and a consistently smooth experience on any device, Power Remote helps presenters focus on their presentation without concerning any other issue besides the content. Behind this smooth and stable experience is the unique Dual-Channel Connection tech and totally automatically-connected function’s thoughfully design. Always designing and developing through a presenter’s view is how they achieved this Best Fan’s Choice award.
